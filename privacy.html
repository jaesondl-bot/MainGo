MainGo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Summary

MainGo does not require you to sign in. We do not sell your data or track you across the web.

What MainGo does

MainGo is a browser extension that opens an official search page on a music service you choose (for example Spotify, Pandora, YouTube, YouTube Music, or Apple Music). MainGo does not play, host, time, or download audio.

Data we collect

MainGo does not collect personal information, account credentials, or browsing history.

Permissions

  • sidePanel — shows the MainGo picker (service and genre).
  • tabs — opens or updates one tab with the official search URL you requested.
  • storage — stores only a session tab identifier so repeated bursts can reuse the same tab. This is cleared when the browser session ends.

Third-party services

When you use MainGo, you leave our extension UI and interact with the streaming service you selected. Their privacy policies apply on their sites.
